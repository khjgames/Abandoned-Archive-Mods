# Abandoned Archive Mao Companion Relic Mod

## Overview

Adds a custom relic called "Mao Companion" to Abandoned Archive. With this relic, for a minor knowledge tax. Mao appears as a companion that assists you.

### Obtaining the Relic

The Mao Companion relic can appear in any relic bookshelf, anarchy reward or boss relic selection, just like any other relic in the game. Pick it up to activate Mao as your companion.

## Features

- **Custom relic** - Adds "Mao Companion" to the available relic pool
- **Companion sprite** - Mao appears and follows the player when the relic is equipped
- **Companion Behavior**: Mao hovers near the player and follows you as you move through rooms

## Relic Details:

### Effect Description
"When charged, Mao fires a projectile at the nearest enemy to your mouse cursor. Mao can fire every 0.25 seconds. Mao gains a charge every 8 seconds and upon every enemy killed. Reduces knowledge gain by 10%."

### Stats
- **Firing Rate**: 1 projectile per 0.25 seconds (when charges available)
- **Charge Rate**: 1 charge per 8 seconds (passive) + 1 charge per enemy killed
- **Knowledge Penalty**: -10% knowledge gain (doesn't apply to gains of 3 or less)

## Installation

1. Place the mao mod folder with the sprites and dll in your `BepInEx/plugins` folder
2. Launch the game - the relic will be available in relic pools 

## Notes

- The relic won't appear if sprite files are missing - make sure both PNG files are in the plugins folder
